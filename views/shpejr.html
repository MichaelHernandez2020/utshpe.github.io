About the Committee

The SHPE Jr. Committee is for any UT-SHPE members who would be part of our bi-weekly visits to our local high school SHPE Jr. Chapters (Del Valle High School and Austin Can Academy). Our goal is to help these chapters flourish and help the students learn more about engineering, college, and their future goals.

If you would like to be a part of this committee feel free to sign up here or contact our SHPE Jr. Representatives Camila Parra Ochoa, Sebastian Garcia, or Eden Garza to learn more.
